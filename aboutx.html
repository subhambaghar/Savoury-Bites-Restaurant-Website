Our Story

Discover the passion behind Savory Bites

Since 2018

Crafting Culinary Excellence

Founded by Chef Subham Baghar in a small downtown space, Savory Bites began as a humble bistro with a big dream. Today, we've grown into one of the city's most celebrated dining destinations, known for our innovative approach to classic cuisine.

Our philosophy is simple: source the finest ingredients, treat them with respect, and let their natural flavors shine. We work closely with local farmers and producers who share our commitment to sustainability and quality.

Subham Baghar
Executive Chef & Founder

2018
